Plant Disease Classes:

BR: Black Rot (causes dark, sunken spots on stems, leaves, and fruits).

RUST: Rust (produces orange or reddish spots on leaves).

PM: Powdery Mildew (characterized by white, powdery fungal growth).

SCAB: Scab (forms crusty or scaly lesions on fruits and leaves).

SPOT: Spot (creates small, discolored spots on leaves and stems).

BLIGHT: Blight (causes rapid browning and death of plant tissues).
